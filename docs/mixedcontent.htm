Mixed Passive/Display Content

to test behavior of Firefox's security.mixed_content.use_hsts + security.mixed_content.send_hsts_priming

** from a domain with HTTP+HTTPS support but without HSTS (cdn.ghacks.net)
==> this should never load, regardless of use_hsts + send_hsts_priming

** from a domain with HSTS support (but without the 'includeSubDomains' directive)
==> this should load with send_hsts_priming;true
       OR with send_hsts_priming;false if you previously visited fe. https://www.heise.de/security/

** from a domain on the Firefox preload-list (agrias.com.br)
==> this should load with use_hsts=true and NOT require send_hsts_priming=true
